What is HEAL?

Heal is an intelligent diagnostic framework designed to bridge the gap between patient-reported symptoms and clinical laboratory data. By correlating physical signs with hematological markers, the system provides high-precision health assessments. Through a transparent logic engine, it transforms complex medical reports into clear, actionable insights, empowering users with early health awareness and informed clinical guidance.
